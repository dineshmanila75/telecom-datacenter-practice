Hyperscale Design

Hyperscale datacenters are purpose-built facilities designed for massive scale, efficiency, and rapid deployment. These facilities power the world's largest cloud providers and are increasingly adopted by enterprises for their own infrastructure needs.

Design Principles

  • Modular, repeatable architecture
  • High-density compute (30+ kW/rack)
  • Automated operations at scale
  • Built-in redundancy and resilience

Key Metrics

  • PUE below 1.1 achievable
  • 99.9999% availability targets
  • Weeks vs months deployment
  • 50%+ CapEx optimization

Modular Datacenters

Prefabricated modular datacenters (PMDCs) offer rapid deployment, predictable costs, and flexibility. These factory-built units can be deployed at the edge, in remote locations, or as capacity expansion for existing facilities.

Advantages

  • Deploy in 12-16 weeks
  • Factory-tested quality
  • Relocatable assets
  • Standardized operations

Applications

  • Edge computing deployments
  • Disaster recovery sites
  • Temporary capacity needs
  • Remote/harsh environments
Essential

  • Energy management & reporting

Advanced Features

  • AI-driven cooling optimization
  • Predictive maintenance
  • Automated compliance reporting
